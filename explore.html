Japanese Chicken Katsu

Japanese Chicken Katsu adalah hidangan khas Jepang yang terdiri dari potongan ayam fillet tanpa tulang, dibalur tepung roti (panko) lalu digoreng hingga renyah dan berwarna keemasan. Bagian luarnya garing sempurna, sementara bagian dalamnya tetap juicy dan lembut. Disajikan dengan saus katsu khas Jepang yang manis dan gurih, serta nasi putih hangat, hidangan ini cocok untuk semua kalangan—baik anak-anak maupun orang dewasa.

Matcha Latte

A matcha latte is a creamy and vibrant green tea-based beverage made by combining matcha powder (finely ground green tea leaves) with milk. The matcha is first whisked with hot water to create a smooth paste, then mixed with steamed milk or a milk alternative. It can be served hot or iced, often sweetened with honey, syrup, or sugar to balance its earthy and slightly bitter flavor.

Berry Smoothie

A berry smoothie is a refreshing and nutritious beverage made by blending berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, or blackberries) with a liquid base like milk, yogurt, or fruit juice. Additional ingredients like bananas, honey, or protein powder can be added for sweetness, texture, or nutritional value. The result is a creamy, vibrant drink packed with antioxidants, vitamins, and fiber.

Mojito

A mojito is a refreshing cocktail that originated in Cuba. It typically consists of white rum, lime juice, sugar, mint leaves, and soda water. The ingredients are combined to create a balance of sweetness, citrus, and minty freshness, making it a popular choice for warm weather. The drink is often garnished with sprigs of mint or a slice of lime for added flair.

Chocolate Cake

Dessert chocolate cake is a rich and indulgent treat made with chocolate or cocoa powder as the primary flavoring ingredient. It typically includes a combination of flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and chocolate, which can be melted or powdered. The cake is baked until fluffy and moist, and it may be layered or topped with chocolate frosting, ganache, or other decorations like fruits or nuts. Variations include molten chocolate cake, Black Forest cake, and flourless chocolate cake.

Apple Pie

Dessert apple pie is a classic pastry dish featuring apples as the main ingredient. It typically consists of a flaky crust filled with sliced apples, sugar, cinnamon, and sometimes other spices like nutmeg or cloves. The pie is baked until the crust is golden and the filling is tender and aromatic. Apple pie is often served warm, paired with vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, or even a slice of cheddar cheese for a unique twist.

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert known for its rich and layered flavors. It typically consists of ladyfinger biscuits soaked in coffee or espresso, layered with a creamy mixture of mascarpone cheese, egg yolks, and sugar. The dessert is often flavored with cocoa powder and sometimes includes a splash of liqueur like Marsala wine or rum. Its name, "tiramisu," translates to "pick me up" in Italian, reflecting its energizing coffee content

Cheesecake

Dessert cheesecake is a rich and creamy dish made with a filling of cream cheese, eggs, and sugar, typically layered over a crust made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, or pastry. It can be baked or unbaked and is usually served chilled. Cheesecake comes in various flavors, such as vanilla, chocolate, or fruit-infused, and is often topped with ingredients like whipped cream, fruit sauces, nuts, or chocolate syrup
